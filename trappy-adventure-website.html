Trappy Adventure

Master the art of timing in this challenging puzzle platform adventure!

Game Overview

Trappy Adventure is an engaging puzzle platform game that challenges players to navigate through increasingly complex levels. With precise controls and thoughtfully designed obstacles, every level presents a unique challenge that will test your skills and reflexes.

How to Play

  • • Use arrow keys or WASD to move your character
  • • Space bar to jump
  • • Avoid traps and obstacles
  • • Collect items to progress
  • • Find the exit to complete each level

Game Features

Multiple Levels

Progress through increasingly challenging stages

Smooth Controls

Responsive and precise movement system

Regular Updates

New content and improvements added regularly
